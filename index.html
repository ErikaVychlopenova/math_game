      How to play

      Your task is to repair the equation by dragging the correct signs in to the correct spots.

      To check if your answer is right, click the button 'Submit answer'. If the equation isn't valid, you cannot continue to the next level.

      If the equation is valid, you can proceed to the next level. There is a total of 10 levels, that are randomly selected.

      The level you are currently on si saved automatically, so if you feel tired and decide to turn off your PC, you can still return to the same level you left on.

      The randomly generated set of levels is saved too.

      Remember: The equation must be valid to proceed to the next level!
